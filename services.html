Dog Grooming Tips & Education

  • Regular grooming helps keep your dog's coat healthy and reduces shedding.
  • Brushing teeth prevents dental disease and keeps your dog's breath fresh.
  • Trimming nails regularly prevents discomfort and joint issues.
  • Bathing with dog-safe shampoo keeps skin and fur clean and healthy.
  • Check ears for wax and debris to avoid infections.
  • Ask our groomers for advice tailored to your dog's breed and age!
